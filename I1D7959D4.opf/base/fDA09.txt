XII. Prayogamārga

punar aparaṃ, bhadanta śāradvatīputra, bodhisattvānāṃ catuḥsmṛtyupasthānabhāvanā apy akṣayāḥ.

katamāś catasraḥ?

tad yathā kāye kāyānupaśyanā smṛtyupasthānam;vedanāyāṃ vedanānupaśyanā smṛtyupasthānām;citte cittānupaśyanā smṛtyupasthān
am;dharmeṣu dharmānupaśyanā smṛtyupasthānam.
